Productivity Optimisation

Real Time Monitoring of Machine Production, Running / Idle / Breakdown / Stoppage time and Energy parameters simultaneously, gives enough data to map the Energy Intensity of each Machine / Process. We have simultaneously improved production and lowered power consumption of large pharmaceutical and automobile industries.

Process

Typical problems with an Industry :

  • Machine up-time and down-time surge issues.
  • Idling and Minor stops.
  • Performance Loss.
  • Equipment failure (i.e. tooling failure, breakdowns).
  • High energy consumption but low efficiency of equipment's.

PROCESS OF INTELLIGENT REAL TIME MONITORING SYSTEM

  • Energy, process and production data is extracted at regular intervals from the manufacturing machines.
  • Data retrieval by NON-INVASIVE TRANSDUCER does not intrude the running system and all the data is collected by our Embedded System.
  • Our intelligent system uploads the collected data to the cloud, where the requisite computations are done for the Energy Flow and Energy Loss at various locations.
  • Beyond the four walls of the confined production facility, the transfer to Cloud-Based software system allows employees and administrators to view the performance status of the manufacturing machines.
  • The graphical representations of the data can be viewed worldwide on our Web / Android / IOS applications.
