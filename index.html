Large Industries

This invention relates to invisible supervision of both energy consumption and productivity output of client enterprises using only one standard product. This product consists of sensor elements (ex. embedded data loggers) which are used to measure only the electric inputs. The Physical data Values are decoded with each Embedded Data logger using Artificial Intelligence Software and stored after condensing and evaluating it. The data is then fed to an Artificial Intelligence enabled software to condition, transform, analyze and predict the preferred outputs. Productivity of the machines is supervised by correlating the machine catalogs and ratings with the Artificial Intelligence software electric data.

Technology Breakthrough

Our Patent (Pending) on "Process Change in District Cooling Systems Energy Transfer Station" is heralded to be a breakthrough in ETS operation. The new process/philosophy of operation of ETS increases the RT/CUM value (Cooling RT delivered per Cubic meter of chilled water) by 10-12% on the Primary side of the ETS, and by more than 40-45% on the secondary side of the ETS. The flow of Chilled Water in the ETS and temperature variations get steadied. The FCV valve movements get minimized, thereby reducing the maintenance costs and down time. In monetary terms, a saving of more than 25-30% per ETS per day is achieved. When applied to a large 40,000 TR District Cooling Plant at Dubai, savings to the tune of 7 million AED were demonstrated.

Invisible Supervision

Our Patent (Pending) on "Invisible Supervision of a Client's Enterprise for Energy and Productivity" is an innovation for invisible supervision of both energy consumption and productivity using only one standard product. This product consists of embedded data loggers which are used to measure only the electric inputs. This system uses AI technologies to find the relationship between Energy and Productivity, thereby avoiding usage of additional sensors on the machines. Invisible Supervision data is untouched and generates alerts automatically For a leading pharmaceuticals manufacturer, machine running times increased by 24%, energy consumption increased by only 10%, energy consumed per unit running time decreased by 40%, and the Return on Investment was less than 6 months.
