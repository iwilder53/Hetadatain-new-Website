Most industries already have BMS/SCADA installed on their system. Data is made available to the operators on a dashboard for control purposes. However, they neither have the skills nor the time available to understand and analyse the data, and take any inferred actions. Heta Datain solution uses available BMS/SCADA data to analyse key energy and performance parameters. Off site teams simulate the critical load sections to understand and eliminate inefficient loss making practices. Detailed reports and technical inputs assist operators to conduct operations smoothly and efficiently.

Productivity Monitoring

Real Time Monitoring of Machine Production, Running / Idle / Breakdown / Stoppage time and Energy parameters simultaneously, gives enough data to map the Energy Intensity of each Machine / Process. We have simultaneously improved production and lowered power consumption of large pharmaceutical and automobile industries.

Energy Optimisation

Energy Optimization and Management System” offers facility owners and operators a comprehensive integrated web based automated energy management program.
